Kyriakides Leonidas

Kyriakides Leonidas Ph.D. ​completed his undergraduate studies at the Pedagogical Academy of Cyprus (Teacher’s Diploma, 1988) and at the State University of New York (B.Sc. 1990, Mathematics). Stephanie Pantelides

Stephanie Pantelides is currently a Post-doctoral Researcher funded by a “Didaktor fellowship” that is co-financed by the European Regional Development Fund and the Republic of Cyprus through the Research and Innovation Foundation and works with Prof. Marios N. Avraamides at the Department of Psychology and at Center of Applied Neuroscience, both at the University of Cyprus. Her research focuses on how people process, represent, and update spatial information encoded through different sensory modalities (e.g., vision, haptics) and language.
